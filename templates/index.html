About Me

My History

I'm Henrique, passionated about mathematics, physics and technology. My first coding experience was C++ when i was 14 years old, unfortunately i couldn't find motivation to continue and stopped working on my coding skills until 2022.

The end of June was nonetheless one of the most important period of my life. Mathematics gave me a new reason to get back into coding since it's fundamental for every modern mathematician to understand the new world and the new industry that already came to us.

After finishing Object Oriented Programming, starting in Django was the right decision, but for me watching tutorial videos wasn't the purpose of learning. Instead, creating my own project was the best thing I had ever done.

The first project was "Gaussian Lair", which was a blog for authorized users to share their knowledge incapsulated within articles, deployed on the "Day Of The Programmer". This work was nothing but the beginning of my path, it has no end and journey, infinite by it's definition, is the learning process and construction of every programmer or aspiring programmer that one day touched the keyboard.

I'm currently working to understand networking better and also learning JavaScript. Since it's a very important language and every back-end developer must learn it.
